What is Local Content?

Local content is the use of locally produced goods, services, labour and expertise in economic activities, particularly in sectors like mining, oil and gas, construction and manufacturing.

In Uganda, Local Content policies aim to empower Ugandan businesses, create jobs and boost economic self reliance.

This Unit implements and monitors local content policies to ensure that foreign investors and companies contribute to economic growth by:

  • Prioritizing Ugandans in recruitment
  • Sourcing raw materials locally
  • Transferring skills and technology
  • Capacity building

We intend to strengthen these efforts by requiring foreign companies to prioritize Ugandan participation in major projects.
